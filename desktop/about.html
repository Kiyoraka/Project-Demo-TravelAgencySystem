About HIJIR

Guiding pilgrims to the Holy Land with care and faith.

We are a licensed Umrah and Haji operator based in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. Since 2024, we have been entrusted by Malaysian families to prepare every detail of their sacred journeys — from flights and visas to hotels and ground handling.

Our small team of mutawwif and tour leaders have served hundreds of pilgrims. We believe every journey begins with trust, and every return home begins with a story worth keeping.

Our packages cover Umrah, Haji, Ziarah, and custom group travel to Madinah, Mecca, Taif, Istanbul, and beyond.

🎯

Our Mission

To ease every pilgrim's path to the Holy Land — with transparent pricing, reliable logistics, and spiritual guidance.

👁

Our Vision

To become Kelantan's most trusted Umrah operator, known for warmth, honesty, and care at every step.

💎

Our Values

Amanah (trust), Ihsan (excellence), and Ukhuwwah (brotherhood) in everything we do.
